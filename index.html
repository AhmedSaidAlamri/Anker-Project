We Are Anker

Anker Management Logistics & services LLC was established in Jebel Ali Free Zone on February 2005 as a branch of the Dubai Head Office and is located near Gate 7 and Gate 8 which is widely considered as strategic location near the Ports and Custom Authority. Our covered warehouses totalling 10,000 sq.m. divided into different sizes each having independent loading bays that can cater to both small sized inventory products to highly voluminous items. We also offer open yard storage of 11,000 sq.m. which is ideal for steel products, pipes and other machineries We aim to provide our clients with the best possible services at competitive rates and with our expertise and dedication we seek to be your complete logistics solution provider.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the logistics leader among warehousing & transport groups in UAE. We are devoted to develop long term strategic partnership with our clients and to provide them a competitive advantage in the market.

Why Jebel Ali?

JAFZA is the world’s largest and fastest growing free zone and considered as having one of the largest sea ports in the world. JAFZA is only 30 minutes drive to Dubai International Airport and is well positioned to enable its partners the fastest possible access to the market. Logistics is all about secured movement of goods, timelines and accessibility which we easily provide due to our strategic location
